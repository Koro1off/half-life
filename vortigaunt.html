The Vortigaunts (taxonomic designation: Xenotherium subservilia,[2] or "Subservient Alien Beast"), formerly known as "Alien Slaves" or "Xen Slaves", and affectionately known as "Vorts" by their allies, are a sapient alien species found throughout the Half-Life series.

They can be seen using their electrical attacks for non-combat purposes several times in the game, such as when charging up a transformer in Black Mesa East and when a Vortigaunt extracts Bugbait from a dead Antlion Guard.

Under certain circumstances, Vortigaunts are capable of manipulating space and time to an impressive degree.[16] In Episode One, a group of purple-colored Vortigaunts use their ability to teleport and rescue Alyx Vance. They also free Gordon from his stasis and are shown blocking the G-Man from interfering using a blue energy shield. In Episode Two, with the aid of a larval extract from an Antlion nest, a group of Vortigaunts are capable of healing Alyx after she sustains near-fatal injuries from a Hunter.
